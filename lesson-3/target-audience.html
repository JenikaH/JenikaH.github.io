Target Audience

The target-audience of the SunnyDayWeather.com web site is described below:

  • Who: Seniors that want to keep well informed of the weather and enjoy sunny day weather.

  • Age: 60+

  • Income:average $48,000 annually

  • Motivation:These seniors motivation is to know when to avoid being outside in bad weather and know when to look forward to the good weather.
